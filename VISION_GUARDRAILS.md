# Pioneer Vision Guardrails

These principles define the identity of Pioneer.

Every product, UX and technical decision should respect them.

---

## G001 — Image First

The captured image is always the center of the experience.

---

## G002 — Session Oriented

Users think in astrophotography sessions, not devices.

---

## G003 — Mobile First

Every feature starts on smartphones.

---

## G004 — One-Hand Operation

Critical controls should remain accessible with one hand.

---

## G005 — Glove Friendly

Touch targets must be reliable during field operation.

---

## G006 — Minimal Cognitive Load

Only relevant information should be visible.

---

## G007 — Two-Tap Rule

Critical actions should require at most two taps.

---

## G008 — Contextual Workspaces

The interface should adapt to the current task.

---

## G009 — Information Over Configuration

Monitoring is more important than settings during operation.

---

## G010 — Native Mobile Experience

Pioneer is not a remote desktop application.
